A/B Test Dashboard

Business question: should an e-commerce company replace its existing landing page with a new design? This dashboard summarizes the experiment, statistical result, rollout risk, and final decision.

Decision Do not roll out the new page
Business Problem
The company needs to decide whether to launch a new landing page.

The current page is the baseline experience. The new page should only be launched if it improves conversion enough to justify rollout risk.

Experiment
Old page vs new page

Users were split into control and treatment groups from 2017-01-02 to 2017-01-24. The primary KPI is conversion rate.

Analysis Task
Clean data, test uplift, estimate business impact

Invalid assignments and duplicate users were removed, conversion rates were compared, and rollout impact was modeled.

Decision Rule
Launch only with clear positive evidence
